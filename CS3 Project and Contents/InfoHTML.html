NEON MIXTAPE TOUR

Neon Mixtape Tour is the 8th world in the game. The world takes place in the 1980s when there were a lot of bands representing a variety of musical genres, such as pop, punk, or metal. Jams, the environment modifier of this world, represent that variety through gameplay. Different from other worlds, the music will make the zombies move faster or slower, and also make special zombies exhibit their special ability. They will only exhibit their special ability if their preferred music or jam is playing. There are six jams in total (more info in Songs).
